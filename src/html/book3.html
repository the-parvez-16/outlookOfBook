Overview

In "Making Sense of Mindfulness," Keith Macpherson offers an accessible, solid, five-step framework that demystifies the buzzword "mindfulness" and provides a legitimate formula to help combat high stress levels and anxieties that plague daily life. The book is designed to help readers integrate and sustain a daily practice of mindfulness, offering tools and techniques to come back into balance and live their best lives.

Making Sense of Mindfulness by Keith Macpherson is a must-read for anyone looking to bring clarity, balance, and peace into their daily life.
